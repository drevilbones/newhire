New Hire Technology Request Site [DEV]

To request network login and email accounts as well as technical resources from the IT Department please fill out this form as completely as possible and hit the submit button. This will generate an email to IT for review and processing. Submitting this request assumes that the appropriate HR paperwork has been completed and signed.

Desired Position Type

To begin, please choose a “Desired Position Type” from the pull-down menu. The pull-down option you choose will then determine what IT resources are available for your choice.

Please note that it is up to the hiring manager to let IT what the new person's login and email address preference should be. For example William Harrison may prefer to have his email address and login read as Bill.Harrison@cambridgecollege.edu and Bill.Harrison.

This will be automatically filled for you based on First Name and Last Name from above, but should be edited to reflect the desired login/email.
